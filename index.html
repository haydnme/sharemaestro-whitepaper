What’s inside

The whitepaper explains how Sharemaestro replaces “indicator soup” with a compact set of proprietary, explainable views that agree or disagree decisively.

  • Market Dynamics - where risk and opportunity concentrate.
  • Smart Money - accumulation footprints, model-qualified buy signals.
  • Demand Threshold - our proprietary exit and short urgency line.
  • Dynamic Momentum - the original momentum snapshot with accumulation/distribution.
  • Momentum Cycle - deviation from the average, plotted weekly for clarity.
  • Market Demand - demand vs price on one canvas: cause and effect.
  • Market Strength - an alternative to crowded RSI 30/70.
  • Market Cycle - context, extremes, and crossovers that matter.
  • Decision Playbooks - early/late campaign, crossovers, and commodity setups.

You’ll also find institutional and retail workflows, case studies (e.g., UNH), risk rules, and a concise glossary for fast onboarding.
